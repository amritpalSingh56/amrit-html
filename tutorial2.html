Olympic Games 2016

Rio 2016 - Brazil hosts summer olympics

The summer olympic Games in RI de janeiro, Brazil, are from August 5 to 21 , 2016 .

The paralympics follow on september 7 to 18, 2016.

Flame Relay starts in Greece

A few months before the games, the olympics Flame Relay will start in Olympic, Greece, where the the inspiration for todays Olympics games originated .

When is the opening Ceremony ?

The Rio 2016 opening ceremony will be at the world famous football (soccer) maracana Stadium in Rio de Janeiro on August 5, 2016
